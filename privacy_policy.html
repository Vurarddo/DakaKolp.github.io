Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15.05.2024

Introduction

Welcome to My Dictionary App. This Privacy Policy describes the policies and procedures on the collection, use, disclosure, and protection of your information when you use My Dictionary App. The app allows you to add custom dictionaries and words, and offers authentication via social networks like Google and Apple.

Information We Collect

  • User Provided Information: When you register through social network accounts (Google, Apple), we collect the information provided by those services such as your name, email address. You can also create and manage custom dictionaries and words within the app.
  • Device and Usage Information: We collect information about how you use our app including the actions you take in your account (such as adding words, creating dictionaries), and data about your device (such as operating system).

How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide and Maintain Our Service: Your information helps us deliver and improve our services, manage your account, and provide customer support.
  • Communication: We use your information to communicate with you about your account and our services. This may include administrative messages and updates about new features.
  • Security: We use the information to enhance the security of our services, combat spam, protect against fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

Sharing Your Information

  • Third Parties: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the app, such as hosting and database management. Information shared with third parties is subject to their own privacy policies and practices.
  • Social Networks: If you choose to register or log in through social networks (Google, Apple), we may share specific information with these platforms to facilitate your access.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to comply with legal processes, prevent fraud, or protect the safety of other users.

Security of Your Information

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet-based service can be 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your information in our possession. You can manage your account settings directly in the app or contact our support for additional assistance.

If you wish to delete your account, you can remove it using "Delete Account" button on Settings screen. After confirming the deletion, the account data will be completely removed and you won't be able to recover it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
